Three structures

Pick the structure that fits your capital and your appetite.

01 · Capital-only investor

You bring capital. Partner brings land & delivery.

For investors who want development upside without operating a build. Partner contributes land at agreed value; you fund the construction; Namou structures the SPV.

You contribute
Construction capital, draw-down per milestone
Partner contributes
Vetted RAK freehold land, delivery management
Typical share
40–55% to capital provider, after waterfall
Exit mechanism
Off-plan sales, refinance at completion, or unit hand-back
02 · Land-only investor

You bring land. Partner funds construction.

For RAK landowners ready to develop but unwilling to debt-finance. You contribute the plot at agreed value; a vetted developer-partner funds the build.

You contribute
Freehold plot at appraised value, in SPV
Partner contributes
Construction capital, contractor management
Typical share
35–50% to landowner, after waterfall
Exit mechanism
Sale of completed asset, refinance to retain, or unit allocation
