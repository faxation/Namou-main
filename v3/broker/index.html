How we partner

Four principles. Non-negotiable.

Fairness

50/50 default split, written in the MOU before any introduction. No haggling after a deal closes, no hidden fees against your share.

Professionalism

Same-day response on broker queries. One specialist owns your deal end-to-end — no handoffs, no internal chains.

Transparency

You see the same data we use — comparables, ROI projections, freehold confirmation. The deck is yours to walk through with your client.

Client protection

Your buyer is registered against you the moment we engage. No back-channel approaches, ever. Your client stays your client.
